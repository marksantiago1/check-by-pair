The Airbus A300-600ST, or Beluga, is a specialised wide-body airliner used to transport aircraft parts and outsize cargoes. It received the official name of Super Transporter early on; however, the name Beluga, for a whale it resembles, gained popularity and has since been officially adopted.

    Length: 56 m
    Introduced: October 1995
    First flight: September 13, 1994
    Cruise speed: 864 km/h
    Engine type: Turbofan
    Wingspan: 45 m
    Weight: 86,500 kg
